Motorcycle FAQ

Your common motorcycle questions answered

What is the best motorcycle for beginners?
Beginner riders should look for lightweight motorcycles with manageable power, such as 300–400cc bikes. Examples include the Yamaha R3, Kawasaki Ninja 400, and KTM RC 390.
How often should I service my motorcycle?
Regular servicing is recommended every 5,000–6,000 km or as stated in your motorcycle’s owner manual. Basic checks like chain lubrication and oil changes should be done more frequently.
Which motorcycle is best for long-distance touring?
Touring motorcycles such as the BMW GS series, Honda Africa Twin, and Kawasaki Versys offer comfortable seating, wind protection, and larger fuel tanks for long rides.
Is ABS important on motorcycles?
Yes. ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) significantly improves safety by preventing wheel lock-up during sudden braking, especially on wet or slippery roads.
What safety gear should every rider wear?
Essential motorcycle safety gear includes a certified helmet, gloves, riding jacket, riding pants, and proper boots. Protective gear reduces the risk of serious injuries in accidents.
